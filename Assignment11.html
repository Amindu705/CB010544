Hotels and Accomodations

There are various options for accomodations in Sri Lanka. There are plenty of tourist hotels in the range of 5 star luxuryness, to affordable, but good quality ones.Also, you can rent a holiday bungalow, or a house, if you are travelling with a lot of people.Also, there are homestays, which is a new concept proposed recently, where you can also have the living experience with the locals.

Hotels

There are a lot of 5 star hotels in Sri Lanka, and most of them are located closeby to major cities, but in some places which are closeby to national parks and forests, there are some of them. Prices are considerably high and in average, one night per person costs about 30 000LKR. But in off seasons, the prices might get lower.But there is no doubt that you get the best in return for the price you pay. Most of those hotels provide ordered tours, so you don't have to worry about planning hikes, safari tours, and other travelling plans.

Bungalow

Holiday bungalows are also a best option if you admire your privacy a lot. You can rent your own bungalow and do the travelling.If you have a lot of people with you, this is the best option.

Homestays

Homestays have been more popluar among tourists because of the affordability and convenience. You can inetrract with local people and get the same experience as a local.Homestays are famous among the backpackers and the people who travel light.Also, you can find them almost everywhere in Sri Lanka.
